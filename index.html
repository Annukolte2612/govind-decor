About Us

Turning Moments into Memories

Sethi Event & Decor is a full-service event styling studio crafting elegant, joyful celebrations for over a decade. From intimate home gatherings to grand weddings and corporate galas, our team blends timeless design with personal storytelling to make every event feel uniquely yours.

We handle every detail — concept, florals, lighting, stage, props and on-site coordination — so you can simply enjoy the moment.
